Account and Data Deletion Policy

At AlFalaah Media Library, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated personal data from our services, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account and Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and all personal data associated with it by contacting our support team. Please provide us with the following information to help us process your request efficiently:

  • Your Name: The name associated with your AlFalaah Media Library account.
  • Email Address: The email address used to register your account.
  • User ID (if available): This can often be found in your app's profile or settings section.
  • Reason for Deletion (Optional): While not required, providing a reason can help us improve our services.

Contact Us:

Please send your data deletion request to:

Email: support@alfalaahmedialibrary.com

We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation once your account and data have been successfully deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon successful deletion request, we will remove or anonymize all personal data directly associated with your account, including but not limited to:

  • Your name and email address.
  • Your account profile information.
  • Your listening history and playback progress.
  • Your saved favorites and downloads list.
  • Any other identifiable personal data collected through your use of our services.

Please note that some non-personally identifiable analytical data or data required for legal and regulatory compliance may be retained, but it will not be linked back to your identity.

Consequences of Account Deletion

Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You will lose access to all features, content, and personalized settings associated with your account. If you wish to use AlFalaah Media Library again in the future, you will need to create a new account.

Thank you for using AlFalaah Media Library.
